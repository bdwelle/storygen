# Narrative Goals

## Tone & Style
- Dark, claustrophobic, intense
- Unflinching in depicting suffering and transformation
- Specific and detailed, not vague or general
- Focus on psychological and physical violation

## Thematic Focus
- Sensual exploration: Raw, visceral, explicit bodily sensations
- Psychological horror: Breakdown of self, loss of control
- Moral complexity: Blurred lines between victim and perpetrator
